# SmartJoint for aerospace control surfaces

**Concept:** SmartJoint can actuate flaps, slats, spoilers or other control surfaces in hybrid-electric aircraft.

## Why SmartJoint?

- **Direct drive** – no gearbox, no backlash, fast response.
- **Optical fiber communication** – immune to electromagnetic interference (critical near power electronics).
- **Low weight** – 400‑500 g per actuator.
- **Absolute position sensing** – no homing needed.

## Status

This is a **conceptual use case**. No physical prototype has been built. The intent is to show that SmartJoint is not limited to ground robotics.

## Next steps (for interested partners)

- Integrate SmartJoint with a small UAV control surface.
- Test torque, speed and response time.
- Compare with hydraulic or servo‑actuated flaps.
